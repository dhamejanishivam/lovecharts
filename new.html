How to Export WhatsApp Chats

  1. Open the chat with your loved one in WhatsApp
  2. Tap the three dots in the top-right corner
  3. Select "More" and then "Export chat"
  4. Choose "Without media" to make the file smaller
  5. Send the exported chat file to us via email or our website

How to Export Instagram DMs

  1. Open Instagram in a web browser (not the app)
  2. Go to Settings > Privacy and Security
  3. Scroll down to "Data Download" and click "Request Download"
  4. Enter your email and password
  5. Select "Messages" under "Select types of information"
  6. Click "Next" and then "Request Download"
  7. You'll receive an email with your data within 48 hours
  8. Send us the messages JSON file from the downloaded data
