How to Access Your Chat History

Follow these simple steps to export your conversations and start creating your Love Chart. Select your platform and device to see specific instructions.

1. Choose your platform
WhatsApp
Instagram
2. Choose your device
Android
iPhone
PC/Web

Open the Conversation

Open WhatsApp and navigate to the chat you want to export.

Access Menu

Tap the three dots in the top-right corner to open the menu.

Select "More"

In the dropdown menu, tap on "More" options.

Export Chat

Select "Export chat" from the options.

Choose "Without Media"

When asked if you want to include media, select "Without Media" (we only need the text).

Share the File

Choose to share via email or save to your device, then upload the file to our website.

Note: The exported file will be in .txt format. Our system will automatically process this to create your Love Chart.

Open the Conversation

Open WhatsApp and go to the chat you want to export.

View Contact Info

Tap on the contact's name at the top of the screen.

Scroll Down

Scroll to the bottom of the contact info screen.

Export Chat

Tap on "Export Chat" option.

Choose "Without Media"

Select "Without Media" when prompted (we only need the text conversation).

Share the File

Use the iOS share sheet to save the file to Files, email it to yourself, or directly upload it to our website.

Note: The export process might take a moment for longer conversations. Be patient and don't close WhatsApp during this process.

Open Instagram

Go to the chat you want to export.

Take Screenshots or Use Screen Recorder

Since Instagram does not support direct export, capture screenshots or screen record your conversation.

Upload Captures

Send screenshots or the video to yourself or upload directly to our platform. We'll take care of the rest.

We recommend trimming long screen recordings for faster processing.

Go to Instagram

Open the app and navigate to the conversation.

Screen Record or Take Screenshots

Use the iOS screen recording tool or screenshot method to capture the messages.

Send or Upload

Use AirDrop, Mail, or Files app to share with yourself and upload to our platform.

Don’t crop out timestamps or usernames. They help us visualize the conversation correctly.

Open Instagram Web

Go to instagram.com and login.

Navigate to Messages

Click the messenger icon and open the desired chat.

Take Screenshots

Use your computer’s screenshot tool to capture the conversation.

Send or Upload

Email yourself or upload screenshots directly to us.
